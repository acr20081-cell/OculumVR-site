Oculum VR is a Western Australian technology company specialising in virtual reality solutions for industry, training, and infrastructure. We design immersive digital environments that allow organisations to visualise, train, and operate with unprecedented clarity.

From offshore training simulations to renewable energy infrastructure modelling, Oculum VR brings complex environments to life with a fidelity that changes how professionals engage with their world.

The Founder

Alfie
Curran-Ragan

Founder & Director — Oculum VR

Alfie Curran-Ragan is a young entrepreneur, technologist, and innovator based in Perth, Western Australia. At the intersection of immersive technology and real-world application, he founded Oculum VR with a singular conviction: that virtual reality would become one of the defining tools of modern industry.

His journey into technology began early, demonstrating an exceptional aptitude for engineering, systems thinking, and entrepreneurial problem-solving. His work has been recognised nationally through Binar X, a pioneering satellite project, and Subs in Schools, a nationally recognised submarine engineering challenge.

Accelerated through Techstars Startup Weekend and West Tech Fest, Alfie is building Oculum VR into the leading provider of immersive technology solutions for the industries that define Western Australia's future.

Project 01

VR
Training

In high-risk industrial environments, the margin for error is zero. Oculum VR builds training simulations that allow workers to rehearse critical procedures, emergency responses, and complex operations within a fully immersive virtual environment — before they ever step into the field.

Realistic simulation of offshore, mining, and heavy industry environments to exact operational specifications

Emergency scenario training including equipment failure, hazardous atmosphere, and evacuation protocols

Measurable competency assessments embedded directly within the immersive training environment

Reduced training costs and significantly increased workforce preparedness and safety outcomes

Project 02

Infrastructure
Visualisation

Before a structure is built, it must be understood. Oculum VR creates high-fidelity virtual environments allowing engineers, stakeholders, and decision-makers to explore infrastructure at full scale — examining every detail, identifying risks, communicating vision with complete clarity.

Full-scale 3D visualisation of wind farms, solar installations, and renewable energy infrastructure

Interactive walkthroughs for project approval, community consultation, and investor presentations

Integration with real terrain and environmental data for accurate site-specific modelling

Enables faster approvals, clearer communication, and reduced design-stage risk across major infrastructure projects
